IBALONG FESTIVAL

ABOUT IBALONG FESTIVAL

Ibalong Festival is a colorful celebration with street dances and performances. It showcases the culture of Albay. Visitors enjoy its lively atmosphere.

HISTORY OF IBALONG FESTIVAL

The festival is based on the epic story of Ibalong. It tells the legends of early Bicolano heroes. It is celebrated yearly to honor their history.

ABOUT BICOL EXPRESS FOOD TOUR

The Bicol Express Food Tour showcases the region’s spicy and flavorful dishes. It is perfect for food lovers. Visitors can enjoy authentic local cuisine.

HISTORY OF BICOL EXPRESS FOOD

The dish “Bicol Express” became popular due to its unique taste. It reflects the region’s love for spicy food. Over time, it became a cultural symbol of Bicol.

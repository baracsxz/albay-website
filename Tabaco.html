ABOUT TABACO CITY MARKET

Tabaco City Market is a lively place where locals sell fresh goods. Visitors can explore and shop for local products. It shows daily life in Albay.

HISTORY OF TABACO CITY MARKET

The market has been an important trading center for many years. It supports local farmers and fishermen. It continues to be a key part of the community.

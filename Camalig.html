ABOUT CAMALIG HOT SPRINGS

Camalig Hot Springs is a relaxing place with natural warm water. It is ideal for отдыха and stress relief. Visitors enjoy its calm environment.

HISTORY OF CAMALIG HOTSPRINGS

The hot springs are naturally formed from underground heat near Mayon Volcano. Locals have used them for relaxation for many years. It later became a tourist attraction.

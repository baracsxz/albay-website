ABOUT DONSOL WHALE SHARKS

Donsol is famous for its whale shark interaction experience. Visitors can swim and observe these gentle giants. It is a unique activity in Albay.

HISTORY OF DONSOL WHALE SHARKS

Whale shark tourism in Donsol started in the late 1990s. It became popular due to eco-friendly practices. Today, it is known worldwide.

FACTS

- Whale Shark Capital

- Season: Nov–June

- Located in Donsol

- Eco-friendly tours

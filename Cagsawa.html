CAGSAWA RUINS

ABOUT THE CAGSAWA RUINS

Cagsawa Ruins is a famous historical site in Albay. It features the remains of a church with a view of Mayon Volcano. It is a popular place for tourists and photographers.

HISTORY OF THE CAGSAWA RUINS

The church was destroyed during the 1814 eruption of Mayon Volcano. Only the bell tower remains today. It stands as a reminder of the tragic event.

FACTS

- Destroyed in 1814 eruption

- Bell tower remains

- Located in Daraga

- Popular photo area

TRAVEL TIPS

- Wear comfortable walking shoes

- Bring a camera

- Visit early for better lighting

- Avoid climbing on ruins

GUIDE

- Take photos with Mayon Volcano

- Walk around the park

- Read historical markers

- Try guided tours

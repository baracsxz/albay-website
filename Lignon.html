LIGNON HILL
NATURE PARK

ABOUT LIGNON HILL NATURE PARK

Lignon Hill Nature Park offers a panoramic view of Legazpi City and Mayon Volcano. It is a great spot for sightseeing and outdoor activities. Visitors enjoy the fresh air and scenery.

HISTORY OF LIGNON HILL NATURE PARK

Lignon Hill was once used as a lookout point during wartime. It later developed into a tourist attraction. Today, it is known for its views and recreational activities.

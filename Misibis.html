MISIBIS BAY

ABOUT MISIBIS BAY

Misibis Bay is a luxury beach resort known for its white sand and clear waters. It offers both relaxation and exciting water activities. It is a perfect destination for vacations.

HISTORY OF MISIBIS BAY

Misibis Bay was developed into a high-end resort to promote tourism in Albay. It quickly became one of the top beach destinations in the region. Today, it attracts both local and international tourists.

FACTS

- Located on Cagraray Island

- Known for white sand beach

- Offers water sports

- Private resort

TRAVEL TIPS

- Book accommodations in advance

- Bring swimwear and sunscreen

- Carry cash for resort activities

- Go during dry season for better weather

GUIDE

- Enjoy water sports like kayaking or jet skiing

- Walk along the beach at sunset

- Try the resort’s local food and spa services

- Explore nearby islands if possible

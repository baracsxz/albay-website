DARAGA CHURCH

ABOUT THE DARAGA CHURCH

Daraga Church is a historic church with beautiful Spanish-era architecture. It is an important religious and cultural landmark in Albay. Visitors admire its design and location.

HISTORY OF THE DARAGA CHURCH

The church was built in 1773 by Franciscan missionaries. It has survived natural disasters and remains standing today. It is now recognized as a National Cultural Treasure.

FACTS

- Built in 1773

- National Cultural Treasure

- Located in Daraga

- Overlooks Mayon Volcano

TRAVEL TIPS

- Dress modestly

- Visit during morning

- Respect church rules

- Keep noise low

GUIDE

- Observe the architecture

- Take photos of the view

- Learn its history

- Attend mass if possible

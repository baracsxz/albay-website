ABOUT HIKING TRAILS AROUND MAYON

The hiking trails around Mayon Volcano offer scenic trekking experiences. Visitors can explore nature and enjoy stunning views. It is perfect for adventure lovers.

HISTORY OF HIKING TRAILS

These trails were originally used by locals and guides. Over time, they became popular among tourists. They are now maintained for safe trekking.

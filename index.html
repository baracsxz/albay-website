PROVINCE OF ALBAY

Albay, located in the heart of the Bicol Region, is famous for its majestic Mayon Volcano, stunning beaches, and rich cultural heritage. Visitors can explore historic sites, enjoy exciting outdoor activities, and savor delicious Bicolano cuisine. With its breathtaking landscapes and warm, welcoming people, Albay offers an unforgettable experience for every traveler.

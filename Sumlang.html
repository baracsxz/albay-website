SUMLANG LAKE

ABOUT SUMLANG LAKE

Sumlang Lake is a peaceful spot with a clear view of Mayon Volcano. It is perfect for relaxing and enjoying nature. Visitors can try bamboo rafting and take photos.

HISTORY OF SUMLANG LAKE

The lake was developed as a tourist attraction by the local community. Over time, it became popular for its scenic beauty and cultural features. It continues to attract both local and foreign visitors.

FACTS

- Located in Camalig, Albay

- Offers Mayon Volcano view

- Popular for bamboo rafting

- Ideal for nature photography

TRAVEL TIPS

- Wear comfortable clothes for kayaking

- Bring a camera for scenic views

- Arrive early to avoid crowds

- Respect the local environment

GUIDE

- Rent a bamboo raft or kayak for a fun experience

- Take photos with Mayon Volcano in the background

- Explore the nearby walking paths

- Enjoy a picnic by the lake

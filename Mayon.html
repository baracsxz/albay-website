MAYON VOLCANO

ABOUT MAYON VOLCANO

Mayon Volcano is famous for its perfect cone shape and breathtaking scenery. It is one of the most iconic landmarks in Albay and offers activities like hiking and ATV rides. Visitors come here to enjoy its beauty and take stunning photos.

HISTORY OF MAYON VOLCANO

Mayon Volcano has erupted many times throughout history. One of its most famous eruptions happened in 1814, which destroyed nearby towns. It remains active and closely monitored today.

FACTS

- Known as the “Perfect Cone Volcano”

- One of the most active volcanoes in the Philippines

- Located in Legazpi City, Albay

- Popular for ATV and hiking activities

TRAVEL TIPS

- Check volcanic activity updates before visiting

- Wear proper hiking shoes if trekking

- Bring water and sunscreen

- Best time to visit: early morning for clear views

GUIDE

- Visit the viewing decks in Legazpi City for photos

- Take an ATV tour around the volcano base

- Hire a licensed guide for safe trekking

- Respect local rules and avoid restricted areas
